KanClaw combines OS-like structure, premium visual restraint and agent-native workflows. The point is not to look “AI”. The point is to make teams, projects and machine-grounded context feel coherent.

Filosofía de diseño

Anti-AI Slop, aplicado de verdad.

This landing intentionally mirrors the app’s discipline: sharp geometry, high contrast, restrained motion, deep blacks, quiet density, no fake startup gradients, no candy-colored “AI” clichés, no rounded toy dashboard language. The interface is the stage. The project data is the lead.

Quiet over noisy Motion exists to clarify depth, not to beg for attention.
Structure over spectacle Every major surface communicates state, hierarchy and consequence.
Grounding over hype Files, memory and runs stay attached to a real workspace.
Precision over fluff Border, spacing, type and contrast do the heavy lifting.
